Site Plan: Bay Area eBiking Club

Building a community of e-bike enthusiasts in the Bay Area

Site Name

Bay Area eBiking Club

This name represents a club that is focused on electric biking in the San Francisco Bay Area. It's short, memorable, and descriptive of the region and the activity it promotes.

Optional Domain Availability: bay-ebike.org

Site Purpose

The site will serve as a hub for the e-biking community in the Bay Area. It will provide:

  • Trail recommendations for e-biking
  • E-bike reviews and recommendations
  • Membership information and sign-up forms

Scenarios

Scenario 1: What is the best e-bike to get for mountainous areas?

Scenario 2: Where can I find contact information for the club's directors?

Color Schema

The primary colors for the site will be:

#3498db (Accent Color)

Used for headings and links.

#2c3e50 (Background Color)

Used for the header background and navigation bar.

Typography

Primary Font: Arial, sans-serif (used for body text)

Secondary Font: Georgia, serif (used for headings)

Sample Text: "Welcome to the Bay Area eBiking Club, your go-to resource for all things e-bike."
